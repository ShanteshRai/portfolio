About Me

I'm an M.Sc graduate in Computational Modelling & Simulation from TU Dresden in Germany, specializing in computational engineering. I'm passionate about solving complex problems with precision and creativity. My interest and specialization spans:

  • CFD Simulations: From aerodynamics to conjugate heat transfer to optimization in multiphase flows.
  • FEA Simulations: Modeling structural behaviour and crash tests.

Here's what defines me:

  • Problem-solving: Tackling challenges across engineering domains with advanced simulations.
  • Curiosity: I'm driven by a need to dive deep into problems and explore the "why" behind every challenge.
  • Collaboration: I believe the best ideas are born when smart minds come together, and I thrive in dynamic team environments.
  • Creativity: Whether designing simulations or approaching problems from new angles, innovation drives me.
